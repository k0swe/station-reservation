Club Shack

Reserve shared stations without the scheduling chaos.

Club Shack gives ham radio clubs a lightweight way to manage shared-station reservations when enterprise groupware is unavailable.

  • Manage each club as its own isolated workspace.
  • Create reservable resources with configurable time blocks.
  • Approve member access before reservations are allowed.
  • Prevent overlapping reservations automatically.
